KAT SHAW

Kat Shaw is a University of Bath graduate with a BSc in Sports Performance, Level 4 Sports Massage Therapy and a certified RockDoc and RockBlader.

Kat has recently returned from Australia where she was coaching rowing and working as a Sports Rehabilitation Therapist.

Kat learnt Kinesiology Taping with RockTape UK at the British School of Osteopathy. Kinesiology Taping is used for structural support, postural support, draining of swelling and bruising and to aid a speedy recovery.

Click the link below to learn more about RockTape and the benefits of kinesiology taping. http://www.rocktape.co.uk/why-rocktape

Kat has a background in Swimming, Rowing and most recently, CrossFit.

Kat runs all our private client treatment sessions in Yatton, Winford and provides home visits
