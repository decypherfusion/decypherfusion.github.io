STTSW

Soft Tissue Therapy South West provides Remedial & Sports Massage Therapy, Spine Manipulation and Sports Trauma Management to treat and manage pain and injuries.

Remedial & Sports Massage focuses on the muscles within the musculoskeletal system.

It looks at the way that the musculoskeletal system works as a whole and focuses on the way that a particular person is compensating for injury or dysfunction through their lifestyle. It seeks to address both the symptoms and the causes of those symptoms.
Remedial massage involves deep tissue massage, neuromuscular techniques, passive and active stretching. It also ‘shares’ relevant movement patterns from physiotherapy and some joint manipulation from osteopathy.

Spine Manipulation can improve joint range of motion and balance tissue and muscle mechanics in order to relieve pain.
