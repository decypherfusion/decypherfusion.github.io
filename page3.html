NICK

Nick is a Sports Therapist having qualifications at level 5 in Remedial & Sports Massage Therapy, Sports Trauma Management (Gold level), Spine Manipulation, Sports strapping & taping and Therapeutic Ultrasound treatment.
Nick specialises in the treatment of sports injuries and the assessment and treatment of postural dysfunction causing pain and discomfort.
Nick has a background in Rugby, Olympic weight lifting and CrossFit and specialises in treating athletes from these and other contact or high intensity sports.
Nick currently provides the pitch side and rehabilitation treatment for Yatton RFC and draws on this experience to treat patients in his private practice in Winford.
